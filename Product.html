FAQ’s

Does this charger work with my EV?
Yes. EvoCharge Home 50 will work with any electric vehicle including Tesla, with the Tesla provided J1772 to NACS adaptor.

Do I need a Level 2 charging station?
Yes. With over 80% of charging happening at home a Level 2 charging station drastically reduces the time it takes to fully charge your vehicle. Charging at home also provides a low-cost and convenient way to charge. The EvoCharge Home 50 provides up to 45 miles of range per hour charged.
To see how fast your vehicle can charge use our EV Charge Time Tool

Is the EvoCharge Home 50 certified?
Yes. The Home 50 has been rigorously tested and certified by Underwriter’s Laboratory (UL).

Do I plug-in or hardwire the charging station?
EvoCharge Home 50 is only available as a hardwired unit.
A hardwired installation is safer and more reliable for high amperage, continuous use electrical products. In addition the 2023 NEC(National Electrical Code) for residential homes requires charging stations over 40A(9.6kw) to be hardwired.

What is the EvoCharge warranty?
EvoCharge warrants each charging station for a period of three(3) years.
For complete details view our warranty policy.

Do I need an electrician to install the EvoCharge Home 50?
Yes. The EvoCharge Home 50 is a hardwired only installation and we recommend a licensed electrician to perform the install. This unit has convenient hardwire access through the bottom of the unit or the optional knockout plug on the back of the unit for a clean look and concealed power connection.
For more details view the install instructions in the product manual.

Can I install the charger outdoors?
Yes, the Home 50 can be installed either indoors or outdoors. The NEMA 4 rated weather-tight (weatherproof) enclosure protects the charger from dirt, rain, sleet, snow, windblown dust, splashing water, hose-directed water and external ice formation. The unit is also IK10 impact rated, one of the highest ratings for enclosures to resist damage from impacts due to harsh environments.

What do I get with the mobile app?
The EvoCharge mobile app is designed to simplify home charging. With the app you will be able to:

  • Schedule automatic charging times for when electrical rates are cheaper
  • Share access to the charger with family and friends
  • Save all your vehicles to your virtual garage
  • Add your utility rates for charging cost estimates
  • View live charging status and past charging history
  • Free access to the online web portal for management, additional data, and reporting

Why can’t I see the chargers Bluetooth signal?
The EvoCharge Home 50 automatically broadcasts the Bluetooth signal for 30 minutes once the unit is powered on. After those 30 minutes to rebroadcast the signal you will need to power cycle the charger by flipping the breaker off and then on again.

Is the Home 50 smart home compatible?
The EvoCharge Home 50 is compatible with Alexa, Siri, and Google Home.
